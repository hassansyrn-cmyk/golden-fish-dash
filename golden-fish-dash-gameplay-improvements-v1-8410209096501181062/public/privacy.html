Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 11, 2026

Introduction

Golden Fish Rush is a casual mobile game developed by Hassan. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our game.

Information We Collect

  • Game Progress Data (stored locally on your device): coins, high scores, unlocked fish skins, settings (sound, music, vibration), shop inventory, daily rewards, and achievements.
  • Leaderboard Data: When you submit a score, we may collect your chosen display name and score via Firebase.
  • Device Information: Basic device type and platform (web or Android via Capacitor) for compatibility.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous gameplay statistics to improve the game experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Save and restore your game progress across sessions.
  • Display your scores on the global leaderboard.
  • Improve gameplay, balance, and features.
  • Provide a personalized experience (e.g., selected fish skin).

Data Storage & Security

Most of your game data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage or Capacitor storage. Leaderboard data is stored securely using Firebase services. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (by Google) for leaderboard functionality. Firebase may collect limited device information as described in their privacy policy. We may display advertisements through ad networks in the future. Any ad-related data collection will follow the respective ad provider's policies.

Your Rights

You can reset your local game progress at any time from the Settings screen. For leaderboard data removal requests or questions about your information, please contact us.
